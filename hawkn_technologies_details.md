## 8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. How do I know what needs to be automated in my business?
During our free consultation, we analyse your daily operations, identify manual and repetitive tasks, check where time is being wasted, and discover opportunities where automation can save cost, reduce errors, and increase speed. We help you understand exactly what to automate for maximum impact.

### 2. I'm not familiar with automation or AI. Can I still use your service?
Yes, absolutely! You don't need any technical or automation knowledge. We break everything down in simple language, show examples, and guide you through how automation will work in your business. You just tell us your problems - we handle the automation part.

### 3. What types of processes can be automated?
Almost any repetitive or rule-based process can be automated. Examples include:
*   Lead follow-ups & sales workflows
*   Billing, invoicing & reminders
*   HR onboarding & attendance
*   WhatsApp, SMS & email communication
*   Data entry & report generation
*   Finance & back-office tasks
*   Inventory & order workflows
*   Chatbots, AI assistants & customer support
*   Robotic Process Automation (RPA)
*   If it's repetitive - we can automate it.

### 4. Will automation replace my staff or increase their workload?
Automation does not replace people - it removes repetitive work, allowing your team to focus on important, high-value tasks. Instead of spending hours on manual activities, your staff become faster, more efficient, and more productive. Automation supports your team, not replaces them.

### 5. How long does it take to implement automation?
*   **Small automations:** 1-5 days (message automation, lead routing, reminders, basic AI tools)
*   **Medium automations:** 1-3 weeks (multi-step workflows, dashboards, integrations)
*   **Larger automation systems:** 1-2 months.
We always give a clear timeline before starting.

### 6. How much does automation cost?
Basic automation starts at $500. Complex multi-step workflows and AI-powered automation are quoted based on the exact requirement. All pricing is transparent - no hidden charges. We design the automation plan based on your budget and business goals.

### 7. Do you offer support, updates, and maintenance?
Yes. We provide monthly, quarterly, and yearly maintenance plans including system monitoring, workflow updates, bug fixes, optimization, and new automation additions. Your automation keeps improving - not just running.

### 8. Do you work with international clients?
Yes! We have clients from India, Singapore, UAE, UK, and more. All automation can be built and delivered completely remotely through WhatsApp, Zoom, and email.

### 9. How can international clients make payments?
We accept payments globally via PayPal, Razorpay International, and Direct bank transfer. All invoices are secure and professionally managed.
